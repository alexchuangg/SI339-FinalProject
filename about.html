About Me

I am currently studying at the University of Michigan, pursuing a bachelor's degree in UX Design with a minor in computer science. I hope to create a stronger connection between technical departments and design usability in order to create products that are equally accessible to all.

Why UX Design?

Entering college, I struggled with finding purpose in my work. In discovering UX design, I found fulfillment right away through both enjoyment in designing and the impact that I could bring to my community. One of my future career goals is to attack the problem of mental health within youth culture.
